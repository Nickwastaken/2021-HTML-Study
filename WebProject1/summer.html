Positives

  • Summer always has the warm weather and heat that many people love making for a great vacation.
  • This is always a great time to go to an ocean or a lake and go swimming and suntanning with your family and friends.

    • Negatives

  • But with summer, there is always hurricanes and bad thunderstorms. Especially on the east or west coast, hurricanes can be brutal.
  • Sometimes the weather can be too hot to do anything, and you'll be constantly sweating which isn't fun.
